Upwtown Vynil Records

An American original, Vynil Records Store was founded by Suss Solomon in 1990. The idea of Uptown developed from a business he founded as a teenager to re-sell 78-rpm jukebox records in his father’s Guayaquil drug store, and it evolved into a record megastore that would change the way we connect with music in the predigital era. From the ‘90s through the ‘2010s Uptownm was the reigning music retailer, an oasis at the intersection of music, art and culture known for its volume and backed by its deep knowledgeable, audiophile staff. An iconic mecca for music discovery, Uptown’s strong visual codes and community philosophy ignited a sense of identity across generations with the mantra: “No Music No Life”. At its peak, Tower grew to more than 200 locations in Ecuador and 3 locations in the USA, and more than 1 billion annual sales. A global brand with a clear and compelling vision.
